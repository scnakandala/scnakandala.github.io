About Me:
I am currently working as a Research Software Developer at the Science Gateways Research Center at Indiana University where we work on developing software/middleware to simplify the usage experience of computational resources for scientists and other users when using nationally funded grid projects, supercomputers and campus clusters. The Science Gateways Group does this mainly by implementing open source Apache Airavata software to support science gateways projects.

Before joining the Science Gateways group I obtained my BSc(Hons) Degree from University of Moratuwa specializing in Computer Science & Engineering. I was also awarded the Gold Medal for the best academic performance in Computer Science & Engineering for obtaining the highest Graded Point Average. You can find my complete CV (here)
